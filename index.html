2) Market Opportunity

The gap between big-order delivery and quick cravings.

Traditional delivery apps are optimized for large restaurant orders with high fees and long ETAs. Quick snack cravings are frequent but underserved. Neighborhood Snacks fills this gap with ultra-local, low-friction delivery within ~2 miles.

  • Primary users: teens/college students, young adults.
  • Secondary users: busy parents, professionals, night-shift workers.
  • Tailwinds: local commerce, gig economy, on-demand expectations.
